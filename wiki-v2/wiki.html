Дух

Тесты на подтверждение

  • Особенность: После того как заблаговоняли призрака первый раз, заблаговоняйте еще раз на 150-170 секунде. Если после этого призрак начинает охоту в течении 60 секунд, то это Дух.
    ✔ Отметить

Тесты на исключение

  • Особенность: Запустите таймер после того как заблаговоняли призрака, если призрак начинает охоту до 180 секунд, то это не Дух
    ✗ Вычеркнуть

Мираж

Дополнительная информация

  • Способность: После телепортации к игроку, на земле появляется показатель ЭМП, которое можно сфотографировать

Тесты на подтверждение

  • Особенность (надёжная): Если призрак проходит сквозь соль, не оставляя следов, то это Мираж
    ✔ Mark Ghost
  • Особенность (надёжная): Поставьте датчик движения и рассыпьте соль как показано на видео, если датчик движения обнаруживает призрака, но нет следов на соли, то это Мираж
    ✔ Mark Ghost

Тесты на исключение

  • Особенность: Если призрак оставил следы в соти то это не Мираж
    ✗ Mark Ghost

Фантом

Дополнительная информация

  • Способность: Создаёт показатель ЭМП на уровне головы игрока, когда использует способность

  • Особенность: Если призрак появляется на фото в журнале, то это не Фантом

  • Особенность: Если призрак мерцает как обычный призрак, то это не Фантом

Полтергейст

Дополнительная информация

  • Способность: Радиус способности разный, в зависимости от размера карты (маленькие карты и средние/большие)
  • Способность: После использования своей способности, создастся показатель ЭМП 2 там, где расположен призрак, а все предметы, которых он кинул, будут иметь показатель ЭМП 3
  • Способность: Может использовать эту способность на одном предмете и даже если свет в комнате включен

Тесты на подтверждение

  • Особенность: Во время охоты, разбросьте вещи так, чтобы призрак мог их кидать, если призрак кидает предметы сильно и далеко, то это Полтергейст
    ✔ Mark Ghost
  • Способность: Положите несколько вещей в кучку, если вещи 'взорвались' (полетели с большой силой), то это Полтергейст
    ✔ Mark Ghost

Obake Blinking
Blinking: During a hunt, the Obake will change models on predetermined blinks: 12, 27, 39, 54, 62, 80, 105, 120, and 132.

This means that the Obake is not guaranteed to shapeshift on hunts that are shorter than about 8s, since the hunt would need to be long enough for the ghost to reach 12 blinks.

See the below video showing the Obake model changes on specific blinks:
Models: The Obake has a pattern that it will follow when changing models. A male model will only shapeshift into other male models, while the female model will only shapeshift into other female models. The pattern for each is the same and the very first model change will always be the same model. The pattern does not reset in between hunts. i.e. if the last model change in a hunt was Grandma, the next model change in the next hunt would be Patient 07.

Male Model Pattern:
Butcher >> Club / Fisherman >> Greybeard >> Naked >> Nerd >> Skeleton >> Brute >> (Repeat...)

Female Model Pattern:
Child >> Grandma >> Patient 07 >> Woman >> (Repeat...)

The Obake will also shapeshift into itself when its own model is the next model in the pattern, which is generally visualized by a reset in posture.

Haunted Mirror
How it Works: Holding the mirror and activating it will show you the location of the ghost's favorite room.
The Cost: Looking into the mirror will drain either 20% sanity or 7.5% per second each time, whichever is higher.
Cursed Hunt: The mirror will break if the user runs out of sanity, whether by using it for too long or attempting to use with a sanity of less than 20%. The mirror breaking will start a cursed hunt.
Notes:
  • While using the mirror, a light source will appear in the ghost's favorite room. Other players can use this to help locate the room.
  • Other players are able to see the room in the mirror while it is being used.

How it Works: The user is able to make wishes to the monkey paw. Wishes can provide information and forced activity, but each wish comes at a cost. Once a valid wish is made, a finger on the paw will curl down and the wish will be granted.
Wishes (Pros / Cons): "I wish to see the ghost"
  • Ghost appears at its current location
  • After the event, a hunt will start and player vision is darkened for the duration of the hunt
"I wish for activity"
  • Doubles all ghost activity for 2 min
  • Breaker set to broken and front door locked for 2 min
"I wish for sanity"
  • Averages everyone's sanity to 50%
  • Sets ghost's favorite room to a random room and increases current sanity drain by 50%
"I wish the ghost was trapped"
  • Ghost locked in its room for 1 min where it cannot hunt, roam, or do abilities
  • Locks doors in the player's room and hunt starts after the 1 min period (25% doors will unlock after grace period)
"I wish for life"
  • Revive a dead player
  • 50% chance that the wishing player will die
"I wish to be safe"
  • Unblocks the nearest blocked hiding spot
  • Breaks the light in that room and ghost is able to sense or hear the player from any distance
"I wish to leave"
  • Unlocks all exit doors (works during hunts)
  • Player slowness and darkened vision for 5 secs
"I wish for knowledge"
  • Removes 1 incorrect evidence from the journal and crosses out associated ghosts
  • Starts a hunt and player vision and audio is reduced the rest of that round
"I wish for [weather]"
  • Changes the weather to the one specified
  • Drops sanity by 25%
"I wish for anything"
  • Randomly chooses one of the above wishes
Notes
  • The number of extended fingers on the hand indicates the number of wishes remaining that can be granted during that contract.
  • Each wish can only be used once per contract. Asking a duplicate question will cause the fingers to wiggle.

Music Box
How it Works: When used, the music box will play music for a time. If the music box is brought within 20m of the ghost, the ghost will begin to sing along, giving away its location but remaining invisible. If the box gets within 5m of the ghost (2.5m for Yokai), the ghost will start an event where it becomes visible and begins to walk toward the box.
The Cost: Being within 2.5m of the music box will drain 2.5% sanity per second while the song is playing, for a total of 75% lost if the music box is allowed to play for its full length.
Cursed Hunt: A cursed hunt will begin if any of the following conditions happen:
  • The player throws the box instead of placing it, causing it to slam shut
  • The player reaches or is at 0% sanity
  • The ghost event (where it is visible and walking toward the box) lasts for more than 5 seconds
  • The ghost reaches the player or the box
Notes
  • It is possible for the music box to close without starting a cursed hunt if either no players are in the investigation area or if the ghost is not close enough to the music box before the music stops playing.

Summoning Circle
How it Works: A player can use a lighter to light the 5 red candles that surround the circle. Lighting all 5 candles will initiate an event followed by a cursed hunt. The ghost will be teleported to the circle to do the event and will wait 5 seconds before initiating a cursed hunt.
The Cost: Each candle will drain 16% sanity from each nearby player when lit, draining a total of 80% when all candles are lit.
Cursed Hunt: The ghost will start a cursed hunt after the summoning circle event.
Notes
  • If the final candle is lit when you have less than 16% sanity, then the ghost will be summoned and skip the event, immediately starting a hunt (not cursed, but can only be prevented by an unused tier 3 crucifix).
  • If the ghost is already hunting when the final candle is lit, the hunting ghost will be teleported to the summoning circle and continue hunting from that location.
  • Attempting to light any candle that is not the last candle without enough sanity will cause the candle to immediately be blown out.
  • The Shade is the only ghost that can appear as a shadow model during the summoning circle event.

Voodoo Doll
How it Works: The voodoo doll has a series of pins. When a pin is pressed into the doll, it forces the ghost to do an interaction. The pin that is pressed is random.
The Cost: Each pin pressed drains the user's sanity by 5% (heart pin drains 10%).
Cursed Hunt: If the pin in the heart is pressed in (or if the player's sanity is at 0% when using the doll), it starts a cursed hunt.
Notes
  • Pushing a pin will drain 5% but have no interaction if the ghost is not close enough to anything to interact with.
  • If a player's sanity is at 0%, all of the remaining pins will be pressed in at once. This will cause multiple interactions (one per pin pressed) to happen at the same time and start a cursed hunt.
